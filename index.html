About Me

My name is Nicholas Garza. Im 24 years old born December 14th 1996. I was lucky enough to be born on the amazing island of Kauai then moved to the great city of Austin, Texas when I was about to 2. After graduating high school 1 1/2 early and haviong some money saved up I decided to make a solo trip back to the island of Kauai. Im fortunate enough to also say my 4 year old son Giovanni was born there as well. As a full time single father in my free time I enjoy spending as much time as I can with my son, watching football, swimming, hiking, and CODING!!!
